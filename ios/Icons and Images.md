* 180px × 180px (60pt × 60pt @3x)
* 120px × 120px (60pt × 60pt @2x)

* 120px × 120px (40pt × 40pt @3x)
* 80px × 80px (40pt × 40pt @2x)

## 	Settings icon size (系统设置图标)

* 87px × 87px (29pt × 29pt @3x)
* 58px × 58px (29pt × 29pt @2x)

* 60px × 60px (20pt × 20pt @3x)
